    📻 Radio Index

    Free live internet radio for NucleoOS. One station list, shared between this app and the Radio app on your Cardputer.

    ＋Add a station. Tap Add station, give it a name and paste a direct stream URL (an MP3 stream). It joins the list everywhere instantly.
    ★Set the device default. The starred station is the one your Cardputer tunes the moment you open its Radio app.
    ▶Listen here. Play any station in the browser. If it serves track info, you'll see the artist, title and a progress bar.
    ⇅Reorder & edit. Use ⌃⌄ to reorder, ✎ to edit, 🗑 to remove. Every change saves to the device the instant you make it.
    Device note: the Cardputer plays plain http:// MP3 streams only — it has no room for HTTPS next to the audio decoder. https:// stations are tagged “browser only”: they play here but won't run on the device. Curated free stations come from SomaFM.
